About Me

I am Emmanuel Ngulube, an innovative leader in agribusiness and technology based in Lusaka, Zambia. With a background in computer science from the University of Zambia (UNZA), I specialize in merging digital tools with modern farming practices to drive efficiency, sustainability, and growth in the agricultural sector.

Through CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I focus on production optimization, supply chain digitization, climate-smart agriculture, and scalable solutions that empower farmers and contribute to food security across Zambia and the region.

Leadership in Agribusiness & Technology

As Founder and Strategic Director of CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I lead initiatives that integrate technology into agribusiness — from precision farming tools and digital marketplaces to sustainable production systems and data-driven decision-making. My goal is to modernize Zambia's agriculture, reduce waste, increase yields, and create economic opportunities through innovation and strategic partnerships.
