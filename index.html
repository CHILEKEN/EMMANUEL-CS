About Me

I am Emmanuel Ngulube, a dedicated leader in agribusiness and technology based in Lusaka, Zambia. Drawing from my computer science background at the University of Zambia (UNZA), I am passionate about fusing digital innovation with practical agriculture to build more efficient, sustainable, and profitable farming systems.

At CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I drive initiatives in precision agriculture, supply chain digitization, climate-resilient practices, and scalable value chains that support farmers, reduce environmental impact, and strengthen food security across Zambia and the region.

Leadership in Agribusiness & Technology

As Founder and Strategic Director of CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I guide the development and deployment of technology-driven solutions for modern agriculture — including IoT-enabled monitoring, digital marketplaces, data analytics for yield optimization, blockchain for traceability, and sustainable resource management. My focus remains on creating measurable value for farmers, partners, and the broader ecosystem.
