About Me

Hello, I'm Emmanuel Ngulube – a passionate youthful agriculturalist, author, politician, and dedicated youth leadership advocate based in Lusaka, Zambia.

I am committed to driving positive change through sustainable agriculture, youth empowerment, and strong leadership. By integrating innovative farming practices with community advocacy, I aim to build food security, inspire young leaders, and contribute to Zambia's social and economic development.

Leadership & Advocacy

As a youth leadership advocate, agriculturist, and emerging political voice, I focus on empowering young Zambians to lead in agriculture, climate resilience, community development, and national progress. My work emphasizes sustainable practices, youth involvement in decision-making, and bridging traditional farming with modern solutions for a stronger Zambia.
