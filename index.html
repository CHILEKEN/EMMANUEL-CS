About Me

I'm Emmanuel Ngulube, a passionate agriculturalist, youth leadership advocate, and innovative entrepreneur based in Lusaka, Zambia. With roots in computer science from the University of Zambia (UNZA), I bridge technology and sustainable agriculture to create lasting impact. Through CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I drive modern farming solutions, empower smallholder farmers, and champion youth involvement in agribusiness for food security and economic resilience across Zambia and the region.

Leadership – CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS

As Founder and Strategic Director, I spearhead transformative agribusiness ventures emphasizing production efficiency, climate-smart practices, digital tools for supply chains, and scalable market linkages. Our mission is to uplift rural communities, optimize livestock and crop systems, and build drought-resilient agricultural enterprises that contribute to Zambia's growth and food sovereignty.
