About Me

I am Emmanuel Ngulube, an innovative executive in agribusiness and technology based in Lusaka, Zambia. With a foundation in computer science from the University of Zambia (UNZA), I specialize in applying digital tools to revolutionize farming practices, enhance efficiency, and promote long-term sustainability in agriculture.

Through CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I lead efforts in smart farming technologies, digitized supply chains, climate-adaptive production systems, and market-enabling platforms that empower farmers, optimize resources, and support Zambia's food security and economic growth.

Leadership in Agribusiness & Technology

As Founder and Strategic Director of CHILEKEN AGRO HOLDINGS, I direct the integration of advanced technologies into core agribusiness operations — including IoT for real-time monitoring, AI-driven analytics for yield forecasting, mobile platforms for farmer-market connections, blockchain for transparent traceability, and sustainable resource management systems. My leadership emphasizes scalable, impactful solutions that deliver value to farmers, partners, and the national economy.
